Why now
The cost of skipping due diligence is rising

HMRC is expanding post-clearance audits on importers — scrutinising origin claims, commodity codes, and declared values, with records reviewable up to three years back. Pair that with rising supplier fraud, and verifying first is no longer optional. These are the red flags our AI search is built to catch:

Hidden litigation recordsActive or past disputes the supplier won't disclose
Fake, borrowed, or inconsistent licensesRegistrations that don't match official databases
Shell companies & identity swapsEntities operating under different names
Poor financial standingWeak balance sheets and payment-fraud signals
